# SWIP - Sam's Wonderful Impressive Card Park

A custom card pack for ROUNDS featuring 148 cards across 3 mechanically distinct classes powered by ClassesManagerReborn. Pick a class entry card to unlock its full card pool, or stay unclassed with 79 always-available cards including a massive silly/chaos pack.

## Classes

### SaucyEnchiladas — Gas, Area Denial, Sustain (NatureBrown)
*"Sustain through gas and area denial. Poison your enemies, heal your wounds."*

Pick the **SaucyEnchiladas Entry** card to unlock 22 class-gated cards focused on poison clouds, healing, lifesteal, and area control.

### ASourFruit — Bounces, Explosions, Chaos (FirepowerYellow)
*"Ricochet chaos. Your bullets bounce, grow, and explode."*

Pick the **ASourFruit Entry** card to unlock 22 class-gated cards focused on bouncing projectiles, stat-on-bounce scaling, and massive explosions.

### Synogence — Fire, Ice, Health, Size Lock (DestructiveRed)
*"Fire and ice. Burn them down, keep yourself standing."*

Pick the **Synogence Entry** card to unlock 22 class-gated cards focused on burn DOT, freeze/slow, size locking, and sustain through fire.

## Installation

Install with r2modman or Thunderstore Mod Manager. Requires BepInEx, UnboundLib, ModdingUtils, RarityLib, and ClassesManagerReborn.

## Bug Reports & Issues

Found a bug or something not working right? Submit an issue on GitHub: [github.com/SLO42/swip/issues](https://github.com/SLO42/swip/issues)

Please include your mod list, what happened, and what you expected to happen. Screenshots or clips help a lot.
