# SWIP - Sam's Wonderful Impressive Card Park

A custom card pack for ROUNDS themed around three brothers — Daniel (ASourFruit), Max (Synogence), and Sam (SaucyEnchiladas). 51 cards with a brotherly, personal, and chaotic vibe. Now featuring poison clouds, burn damage, laser beams, orbital strikes, missile barrages, and a Mythic-rarity boss card!
